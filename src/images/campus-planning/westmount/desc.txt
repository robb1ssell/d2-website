Westmount

This proposed CCRC is split into 3 phases. Phase 1 is designed to have 228 total Living Units with a mixture of Independent Living apartments, Assisted Living apartments, Skilled Nursing units, and Memory Support care. Phases 2 and 3 are designed to have 372 total units with several neighborhoods of Independent living cottages.

Location: Maryland
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Master Plan
