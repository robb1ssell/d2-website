Woodbridge

A proposed CCRC on a heavily wooded site and flanking a 9-hole golf course. The CCRC was designed to include 300 Independent Living Apartments, 28 Independent Living Cottages, 20 Assisted Living Units, 45 Skilled Nursing Units, and 40,000 square feet of amenity space for residents.

Location: Woodbridge, CT
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
