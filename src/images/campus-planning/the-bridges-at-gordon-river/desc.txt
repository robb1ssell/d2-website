The Bridges at Gordon River

This project is designed as a community that implements the best features of an Italian village. In its completed form, the CCRC will house 443 resident apartment homes with varying levels of care: Independent Living, Assisted Living, Skilled Nursing, and Memory Support. The commons building is centrally located, and features 3 levels of amenities.

Location: Naples, Florida
Lead Architect: David Dillard, F AIA, Doug Bissell, AIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Total Square Footage: 1,000,000
