Master Plan (in process)

This project is a large repositioning master plan that incorporates all levels of care. New construction is phased to include 90 new assisted living units, new public amenities, and 70 independent living apartments. The project also includes a complete renovation of a 238 bed skilled nursing facility into small-house style households, and 4 new memory support houses with 16 units each.

Location: Witheld upon client request
Lead Architect: Grant Warner, AIA
Type: CCRC – Repositioning Project
Total Square Footage: 364,000
