Master Plan (in process)

This project is a full CCRC that is part of a larger urban master plan with retail, office, restaurant and assembly spaces. It is a multi story complex tailored to a small urban space. 201 Independent living apartments occupy three floors. Adjacent to the independent living building is a 7 story healthcare building with 40 assisted living units, 50 memory support units, and 95 skilled nursing units.

Location: Witheld upon request
Lead Architect: David Dillard, F AIA, Jesse Rodriguez, AIA
Type: CCRC Masterplan
Total Square Footage: 578,800
