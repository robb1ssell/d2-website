The Buckingham

The Tower at Buckingham will soon add 187 new residences  that will include 104 independent living apartments, 33 assisted living suites, 18 memory care residences and 32 private skilled nursing rooms. The independent living units will consist of one-, two- and three-bedroom plans. The expansion will include upgrades to existing common areas as well as additional amenities and services.

Location: Houston, TX
Construction Start: Fall 2015
Type: CCRC
Unit Count: 187
Total Square Footage: 333,000 SF
Contractor: Andres Construction
