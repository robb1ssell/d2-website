Buckner Villas is an existing Life Plan Community in Austin, Texas.  Phase Two of a repositioning plan, this expansion and renovation project added 69 one- and two-bedroom apartment homes and 70 underground parking spaces. It also includes a renovation of the common areas by enlarging the dining room, extending the bistro, adding a theater, broadening the salon and the spa, building a larger meeting space, as well as refreshing décor and interior design throughout. The project includes 122,331-square-foot expansion and 16,010-square-foot remodel.
