Querencia at Barton Creek

A 38 acre development nestled in the heart of the Texas Hill Country, Querencia’s campus was master planned by the D2 Staff to compliment its neighbors – the world class Barton Creek Country Club and Resort, a prestigious single-family neighborhood, and Barton Creek Retail (also designed by D2 Staff). “Cool Springs Spa” and wellness center is a destination for health conscious, independent living residents.

Location: Austin, TX
Year Built: 2007
Lead Architects: David Dillard, FAIA, Doug Bissell, AIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Unit Count: 163 Independent Apts, 103 Healthcare
Total Square Footage: 460,000
Contractor: SpawGlass
