Harbor’s Edge

This 16 story waterfront retirement community is part of a larger brownfield reclamation project over- looking the harbor. The community’s assisted living and skilled nursing units are on the upper floors of the 4 story building with easy access to community activities at the ground level. The 16 story tower includes two levels of parking, a floor with dining venues, and 13 floors of independent living.

Location: Norfolk, VA
Year Built: 2007
Lead Architects: Doug Bissell, AIA David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Unit Count: 163 Independent Apts, 83 Healthcare
Total Square Footage: 450,000
