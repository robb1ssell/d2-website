The Legacy Midtown Park is a rental CCRC located in Dallas, Texas.  It will have 130 units of Independent Living and 3 guest units, 45 Assisted Living units, 30 Memory Care beds and 40 Skilled Care beds. One level of subterranean parking for the Independent residence and health care staff will be provided below the auto court and terrace. The site challenges will be handling the varied topography along a very slender site as well as creating an urban edge to meet the requirements of the Planned Development.

Some of the amenities include the following;

Sanctuary to accommodate 200+ people
Wellness center with lap pool and spa
A destination pub with outdoor terrace
A Bistro that will anchor a private terrace with views to Royal Oak Country Club
Semi-formal and formal dining
Skylounge
