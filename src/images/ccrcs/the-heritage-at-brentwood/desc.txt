The Heritage at Brentwood – Phases 2 & 3

The Heritage at Brentwood includes unique amenities to enrich the residents’ lifestyle. Phase Two added 37 villas complete with a community garden to enhance the neighborhood feel. The third phase will add Independent Living apartments, new amenities, Skilled Nursing units, Assisted Living units, and Memory Support units.

Location: Brentwood, TN
Year Built: (Phase 2) 2007
Lead Architects: Grant Warner AIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Total Square Footage: 77,000 (Phase 2); 300,000 (Phase 3)
