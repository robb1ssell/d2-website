The Stayton at Museum Way

This new CCRC represents a major addition to the places for seniors to call home on the west side of Fort Worth, Texas. The eleven story building is comprised of Independent Living and amenity spaces, including a full spa, indoor pool and a wellness center, as well as various other amenities designed to encourage a healthy lifestyle for each resident. Also included are three dining venues, all of which have views facing downtown Fort Worth, Trinity Park, and the Museum District.

Location: Fort Worth, TX
Year Built: 2011
Lead Architects: Doug Bissell, AIA, David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Unit Count: 188 Independent Apts, 97 Healthcare
Total Square Footage: 415,000
Contractor: Andres
