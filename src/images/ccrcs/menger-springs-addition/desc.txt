Addition – Independent and Assisted Living Residences 

Nearly complete, the addition of independent living and assisted living residences along with various interior renovations will enhance the community at Morningside Ministries’ Menger Springs. The addition includes a four-story, 63-unit independent living building and a 42-unit expansion to the assisted living facilities, including commons and amenities. The independent living building, known as The Overlook will feature a grand hall for events and social activities.

Location: Boerne, TX
Year Built: 2015
Lead Architect: Grant Warner, AIA
Type: CCRC Addition
Total Square Footage: 150,000
Contractor: Koontz Corporation
