Montereau in Warren Woods - Phase 2

This master plan involved maximizing the 172 acre site and increasing the amenities provided to the residents of this existing resort-style community. The project included concurrent design of several multi-phased additions to the campus maintaining a French design theme. The addition includes new Independent Living apartments, an Assisted Living wing, and alterations to the existing Skilled Nursing wing which includes a Physical Therapy suite, a Memory Support addition, and an amenity addition.

Location: Tulsa, OK
Year Built: 2012
Lead Architects: Josh Williams, AIA, Grant Warner, AIA
Type: Continuing Care Retirement Community
Unit Count: 62 IL apts., 27 Memory Support, 22 Assisted, 10 Skilled
Total Square Footage: 206,000
Contractor: Flintco
