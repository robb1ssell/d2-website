On the Eastern edge of Texas hill country, the Barton Creek Retail center is a welcome addition to its neighbors. There is a very symbiotic relationship between the office/retail center and its immediate neighbor – Querencia, a large new senior senior living community with a population of approximately 400 residents, most ambulatory and able to enjoy the dining, retail, and even office opportunities offered.

Location: Austin, TX
Year Built: 2005
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Retail
Total Square Footage: 129,000
