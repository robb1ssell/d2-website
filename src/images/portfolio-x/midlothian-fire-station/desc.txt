The brand new 8068 SF City of Midlothian Fire Station #3 consists of three apparatus bays, and sleeping dormitories for a peace officer and six firefighters. The station also has an “overflow” sleeping area for guest firefighters, fully equipped shower rooms, a living room, full working kitchen, and an area for the firefighters to exercise.

Location: Midlothian, TX
Year Built: 2008
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Civic
Total Square Footage: 8,060
