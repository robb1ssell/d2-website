The Pradera Clubhouse

D2 Architecture was invited by Criterion Development Partners to be the specialty design consultant on The Pradera in Richardson, Texas. In that capacity, D2 designed holistically the central club house and recreation buildings (shown) and prepared general elevation studies for BGA, the architect-of-record, to use throughout the balance of the project.

Location: Richardson, TX
Year Built: 2009
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Multi-Family
Total Square Footage: 10,000
