Greenwood Townhomes are comprised of 9 town homes, totaling 30,000 SF, in the State Thomas neighborhood of Dallas, Texas. Sited to be built adjacent to a historic cemetery, the,project is designed as a contemporary interpretation of historic Texas architecture. Each unit features large windows highlighting views of the Dallas skyline and a covered rooftop patio with an outdoor fireplace. In addition to the units, the D2 team designed a trellis, water feature and pedestrian walkway to enhance the site.
