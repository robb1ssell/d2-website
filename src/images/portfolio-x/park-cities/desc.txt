Park Cities Townhomes is a 1.7 acre development of 36 townhomes in Dallas, Texas. The gated community was designed to compliment the prestigious residential neighborhood. The architecture is reminiscent of the bungalow style homes in the 1940’s. Two and three story units range in size from 1700 to 2200 SF. Each features vaulted ceilings, balconies, an attached garage ,and backyards. The community also has many other amenities, including an in-ground pool and lounge area.

Location: Dallas, TX
Year Built: 2007
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Residential
Total Square Footage: 82,000
