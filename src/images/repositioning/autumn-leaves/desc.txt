Autumn Leaves
D2 was charged with updating the architecture of this existing CCRC in a historic area of Dallas near White Rock Lake. The addition of exterior canopies, shaded outdoor decks facing the lake, fountains, and awnings adds a contemporary and inviting feel to Autumn Leaves, while at the same time retaining it’s original character.

Location: Dallas, TX
Year Built: 2012
Lead Architect: David Dillard, FAIA, Jesse Rodriguez, AIA
Type: Renovation & Exterior Improvements
