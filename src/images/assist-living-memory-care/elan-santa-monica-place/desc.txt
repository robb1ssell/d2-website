Élan – Santa Monica Place is a new Assisted Living and  Memory Support (MS) community on approximately 4.6 acres in Albuquerque, NM. The Community includes new 1 and 3 story buildings containing 77 licensed Assisted Living (AL) apartments and 32 licensed assisted living Alzheimer’s Certified memory care residences.  The project includes amenities and services spaces, with fitness center, salon, food services, dining and other amenities.  Each community if the Élan portfolio reflects the environment in which it resides and Élan Santa Monica is no exception. The building gently alludes to both the artistic community and the surrounding mountains.
