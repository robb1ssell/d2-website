Prestonwood Court

Prestonwood Court is located on four acres of land in Plano, TX. The New England Bed and Breakfast style architecture houses an indoor pool, salon, fitness center, bistro, and country kitchen. There are two levels of care offered at Prestonwood Court- Assisted Living located on three levels, and one Memory Support household in a single story wing.

Location: Plano, TX
Year Built: 2006
Lead Architects: Grant Warner, AIA, David Dillard, FAIA
Type: Assisted Living and Memory Support Community
Unit Count: 124 Healthcare
Total Square Footage: 117,000
